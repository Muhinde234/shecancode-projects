About Me

Get to know me

Hi there! I’m a passionate UI/UX designer and frontend developer with a strong focus on creating responsive, accessible, and user-friendly applications. I enjoy crafting clean interfaces and turning creative ideas into fully functional products that solve real-world problems.

Design isn’t just about making things look good it’s about how they work. I love digging into user behavior, simplifying complex flows, and building designs that feel intuitive. Whether it's wireframing in Figma or prototyping new ideas, I aim to design with empathy and purpose.

As a frontend developer, I work mostly with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS. I pay close attention to detail and enjoy the process of transforming designs into interactive, real-life interfaces. I believe in writing clean, reusable code that’s easy to scale and maintain.
